My Favorite Shows

By Joshua Lee Petersen

#1 - Young Justice

Young Justice takes the top spot due to its exciting plot, interesting characters,
and exceptional continuity across multiple seasons. It is truly a masterpiece
of the superhero world.

#2 - Hunter x Hunter

Hunter x Hunter is a classic anime series that follows the adventures of a young
boy named Gon. The unique settings, likeable characters, and interesting concepts
make this a fun watch.

#3 - Gravity Falls

Gravity Falls is a mystery show based in the little town of Gravity Falls. The wacky
humor and its mysterious plot makes the show fun and intriguing.
